Delving into the Depths of Cognitive Learning:

This innovative tool, made by Jithin Vinod (20ME31020), empowers you to seamlessly classify questions based on the esteemed Bloom's Taxonomy. By meticulously analyzing each question, it discerns its alignment with the six fundamental levels of intellectual engagement:

  • Knowledge : Recalling specific facts or knowledge
  • Comprehension : Grasping the meaning and implications of information
  • Application : Employing knowledge to solve problems and make decisions
  • Analyze : Breaking down complex information into its components
  • Evaluation : Assessing the validity and significance of information
  • Synthesis : Generating new ideas and solutions

Model Training: The models were trained on a dataset of about 2000 questions. The final prediction was decided on by accounting for the predictions of each model and iteratively assigning them weights, which were implemented in the final tool.
